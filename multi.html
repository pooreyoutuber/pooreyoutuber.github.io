How It Works

Free Online PDF Crop Tool — No Login, No Server, 100% Private

The PDF Crop Tool lets you trim any area of your PDF document directly inside your web browser — no software to install, no account to create, and your file never leaves your device. Using this tool is as simple as uploading your PDF, drawing a rectangle over the area you want to keep, and clicking download. Within seconds, you have a clean, professionally cropped PDF ready to save or share.

Whether you need to remove unwanted margins from a scanned document, trim the header or footer from a payment receipt, cut out a specific section of a contract for review, or clean up a multi-page report before sending it to a client — this tool handles it instantly and completely free.

📖 How to Use — Step by Step

01

Upload Your PDF

Click "Choose PDF File" or drag and drop your PDF onto the upload area. The file is loaded instantly in your browser — nothing is sent to any server.

02

Navigate Pages

Use the Previous / Next buttons in the toolbar to browse through all pages of your PDF. Click any page in the sidebar panel to jump to it instantly.

03

Draw Your Crop Area

Click and drag directly on the PDF canvas to draw your crop selection rectangle. You can see the exact pixel coordinates of your selection in the sidebar.

04

Zoom If Needed

Use the + Zoom and − Zoom buttons to get a closer look at your PDF before drawing your selection. Perfect for documents with small text or fine details.

05

Apply to One or All Pages

Choose whether to apply your crop selection to only the current page, or to all pages in the document at once using the toggle buttons in the sidebar.

06

Download Your PDF

Click "Apply Crop" then "Download Cropped PDF" to save your trimmed document. The final file downloads directly to your device in standard PDF format.
