What this tool actually is

PooreYoutuber's SEO Audit Tool is a bridge — nothing more. We do not run our own crawler, we do not generate performance scores, and we do not host, mirror, or reproduce Google's audit engine in any form. When you enter a URL above, we simply construct the correct request and route you to pagespeed.web.dev, which is a free public tool built and operated entirely by Google. Every number, every recommendation, and every score you see on the resulting report is calculated, generated, and served by Google's own Lighthouse and Core Web Vitals infrastructure — not by us.

We built this page because typing a full URL into PageSpeed Insights, waiting for it to load, and navigating its interface can feel like an extra step when you're checking several sites back to back. This tool exists purely to save you that step: type a domain here, and we take you directly to your report on Google's official platform, the same way a shortcut on your desktop takes you to an app you already trust. Think of us as a signpost pointing down the road, not the destination itself.

Because of this, we take on no responsibility whatsoever for the accuracy, availability, completeness, or interpretation of the audit results shown after you are routed. If PageSpeed Insights is temporarily down, changes its scoring methodology, rate-limits a request, or returns an error for a particular URL, that is entirely between you and Google's service — we have no control over Google's infrastructure and cannot guarantee uptime, accuracy, or consistency of the third-party report. Similarly, any decisions you make based on the SEO or performance data shown — redesigning a page, changing hosting, adjusting scripts, or anything else — are decisions you make independently, using Google's data, at your own discretion and risk.

We do not store the URLs you submit, we do not log your audit history on our servers, and we do not sell, share, or otherwise use the websites you test for any purpose beyond generating the redirect link in that single moment. No account, email, or personal information is required to use this page, and none is collected by us through this tool.

If you are looking for an official, authoritative, and fully supported SEO and performance testing tool, we always recommend going directly to pagespeed.web.dev or Google's Search Console, and treating this page as exactly what it is: a fast, free, minimal shortcut that gets you there a few clicks sooner.
