Click any detected text to edit it

Edit text right on your PDF

Upload a PDF and it'll detect the text automatically — click on any line to edit it, add new text or images, or white-out content. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Good to know: edited/added text uses a standard font (it won't always match the original PDF's exact font). Table/graphic-heavy pages, rotated pages, and scanned (image-only) PDFs may not line up perfectly. Cropping and page-splitting aren't included in this version. Everything stays on your device — nothing is uploaded anywhere.
