Good to know

  • Works on text-based PDFs. Scanned pages (photos of text with no selectable text layer) will come through blank — this tool doesn't do OCR.
  • Downloads as a real .docx file (proper OOXML), not an HTML file wearing a Word extension.
  • Simple grid-style tables are detected and rebuilt as real Word tables. Detection is heuristic — merged cells, nested tables, or tables with irregular spacing may still come through as plain text.
  • Larger text is promoted to Word heading styles automatically, based on relative font size.
  • Images, charts, form fields, and exact font matching are not carried over — this tool works with text and simple table layout only.
  • Everything runs locally in your browser tab — your PDF is never uploaded anywhere.
