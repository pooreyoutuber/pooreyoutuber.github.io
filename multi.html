How Does This SEO Audit Tool Work?

            
Unlike typical SEO checkers that only guess or simulate results, SEOPulse uses Claude AI to actually fetch your page's HTML through a CORS proxy, parse its real content, and run a genuine structural audit. Every metric shown — your title tag, meta description, heading counts — comes from your actual live webpage, not estimations.

            
When you submit a URL, the tool fetches the raw HTML, extracts all relevant SEO signals (meta tags, heading hierarchy, Open Graph tags, canonical links, robots directives, image alt attributes), and sends this parsed data to Claude AI for intelligent analysis. Claude then evaluates each signal against current best practices and generates specific, actionable recommendations tailored to what it actually found on your page.

            
Why Real Data Matters

            
Simulated audits can tell you generic rules, but they cannot tell you what's actually wrong with your site. Real audits catch issues like duplicate H1 tags that only appear in your specific template, descriptions that exceed Google's 160-character cutoff by just a few words, or missing canonical tags that are causing index bloat. Every recommendation here is derived from your actual page content.

            
Scoring Methodology

            
The overall SEO score is calculated from six weighted signals: Meta Title quality (20%), Meta Description quality (20%), Heading structure (20%), HTTPS security (15%), Image alt text coverage (15%), and additional metadata like Open Graph and canonical tags (10%). Each signal is scored from 0–10 and the weighted average produces your final score out of 10.
