HOW TO PLAY & GAME DESCRIPTION

Welcome to the Ultimate Browser-Based CS Style Aim Trainer! Yeh game khas taur par Counter-Strike 2 (CS2) aur Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CSGO) ke reflex aur muscle memory training mechanics ko dhyan me rakh kar banaya gaya hai. Video me dikhaye gaye design ki tarah, is game me ek custom horizontal 3D perspective projection track hai, jo piche se aage ki taraf move hota hai (conveyor belt style). Is track par wooden targets continuous speed se chalte hue aapki screen ki taraf aate hain. Aapka main objective in moving targets ko minimal bullets me accuracy ke sath shoot karna hai.

Core Mechanics aur Controls: Game ka mechanical control behatar banane ke liye isme automatic mouse movement handling di gayi hai. Jaise hi aap game screen ke andar apna mouse move karenge, canvas par bana custom crosshair aur 3D gun model (right side me positioned) real-time me smooth follow up karega. Shoot karne ke liye aapko Left Mouse Click ka use karna hoga. Har click par gun se fire hoga, ek automatic muzzle flash generate hoga aur recoil animation active hogi jo aapko real first-person shooter (FPS) ka experience degi.

Scoring aur Precision System: Yeh trainer sirf click karne ke baare me nahi hai, balki precision par focus karta hai. Jab aap kisi target par crosshair rakh kar fire karte ho, toh aapko unki placement ke mutabik points aur dynamically feedback milta hai:

  • PERFECT (100 Points): Agar aapka shot target ke bilkul center body par lagta hai. Isse aapka score tezi se boost hoga.
  • GREAT (50 Points): Agar shot center se thoda side me lagta hai par target hit ho jata hai.
  • GOOD (20 Points): Agar shot target ke bilkul edges/corners par lagta hai.

Ammo, Time Management aur Game Over: Game ko challenging banane ke liye aapko ek standard CS magazine milti hai jisme 30 Bullets hoti hain. Har ek shot par ammo kam hogi, lekin ghabraiye mat—jaise hi aapki magazine 0 par pahunchegi, gun automatic reload mode me chali jayegi (kuch milliseconds ke liye firing lock ho jayegi aur magazine fir se full ho jayegi). Game total 45 Seconds tak chalta hai. Time end hone par game automatically finish ho jayega aur screen par aapki total accuracy fee, hit percentage aur final score ka dashboard open hoga.

Pro Training Tips: Apne reflex ko behtar karne ke liye targets ke movement pattern ko track karein. Unhe continuous spray karne ke bajaye crosshair ko smooth tap-fire ke sath align karein. Apni dynamic accuracy ko 90% se upar rakhne ka prayas karein. Yeh tool aapke reaction time ko drastic tarike se improve karega jisse aap direct competitive matchups me headshots connect kar payenge!
