Why Convert PDF to Image?

PDFs are great for maintaining formatting across different devices, but they aren't always the most "social-media friendly" or "web-embeddable" formats. By converting a PDF to a PNG or JPG, you unlock the ability to share content on platforms that do not support document uploads. Furthermore, images are often faster to load in web galleries and easier to manipulate in graphic design software like Canva or Photoshop.
