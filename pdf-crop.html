PDF Crop Tool — Trim Any PDF in Your Browser, No Login Required

The PDF Crop Tool lets you visually select and remove unwanted areas from any PDF document — directly inside your web browser. Upload your file, draw a rectangle around the content you want to keep, and download a clean cropped PDF in seconds. Your file never leaves your device — everything runs locally with no server, no account, and no cost.

Ideal for trimming margins from scanned receipts, removing headers or footers from documents, cutting out a specific section from a multi-page report, or cleaning up any PDF before sharing or printing.

📖 How to Use

1

Upload PDF

Click "Choose PDF File" or drag your file onto the upload area. Loads instantly in your browser.

2

Draw Crop Area

Click and drag directly on the PDF preview to draw a blue selection box around the area you want to keep.

3

Zoom If Needed

Use + / − buttons to zoom in for precision selection on documents with small or dense content.

4

Choose Pages

Toggle "Current Page" or "All Pages" to apply your crop to just one page or the entire document at once.

5

Apply & Download

Click Apply Crop, then Download. Your trimmed PDF saves directly to your device in seconds.
