5 Tips for Writing Viral Reel Captions

1. Start with a Hook: The first line of your caption must grab attention. Use curiosity, a bold statement, or a relatable problem.

4. The Call to Action (CTA): Don't just post and ghost! Tell your viewers exactly what to do—"Follow for more," "Check the link in bio," or "Tag a friend who needs this."
