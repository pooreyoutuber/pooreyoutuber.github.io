How to Use the Tool

Using PureAudio is incredibly straightforward and involves just a few basic steps. First, prepare your video file on your device. To upload, simply click on the dashed upload box to open your file browser and select your video, or drag and drop the file directly into the designated area. Once selected, the interface will automatically display the file name and size for your verification.

After verifying your file, click the dynamic "Extract MP3 Audio" button. The tool will instantly start separating the audio stream from your video. A live progress bar will appear to keep you informed of the conversion status. Once the process reaches completion, a prominent green download button will appear. Click it to save the freshly generated MP3 file straight to your local storage. It is that simple, taking only a matter of seconds from start to finish.
